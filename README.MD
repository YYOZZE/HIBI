# HIBI · Product Overview (Customer Edition)

**HIBI** is a **cross-device assistant app** for daily work and study. It runs on **Windows, Android, iOS**, and more. You can capture ideas, manage your schedule, chat with an AI assistant, and send files quickly over the local network—data can stay on the device only, and after sign-in it can also sync across your devices.

---

## Who is it for?

- Anyone who wants to **jot down ideas and sketch simple diagrams**  
- Anyone who wants **schedule and reminders in one place**  
- Anyone who wants a **conversational digital assistant** (Q&A, light organization)  
- Anyone who often **sends files between phone and PC on the same Wi‑Fi**  

---

## What can it do?

### 1. Mind canvas  
Turn thoughts into **nodes and links**: project list, blocks and arrows inside each project, zoom and drag—handy for flows, outlines, or relationships. Good for brainstorming and lightweight notes.

### 2. Schedule  
Add, edit, and remove **events**, with recurrence support; reminders can be tied to items on the canvas (follow in-app flows). Data is stored locally; after sign-in it can sync to your other devices.

### 3. Digital assistant  
Create multiple **assistant personas** and chat with AI; copy a single message, delete one or many, to keep history tidy. Requires network and backend for large-model features.

### 4. LAN transfer  
On the same network, **discover nearby devices**, **send files or text**, and view a transfer log. Useful when you prefer not to use cloud or complex setup. (On Windows, if discovery fails, check firewall rules for the app’s UDP ports.)

### 5. Account & sync (optional)  
- **No sign-in required**: use everything as a **local account**; data stays on the device.  
- **After sign-in**: mind, schedule, and assistant data can **merge and sync** across devices; sign-up, sign-out, and switching accounts are supported, with separate on-device copies per account so data does not mix.

## 适合谁用？

- 需要**随手记下想法、画简单结构图**的人  
- 希望**日程与提醒**集中在一处的人  
- 想有一个**可对话的数字助理**（问答、辅助整理）的人  
- 经常在同一 Wi‑Fi 下**手机与电脑互传文件**的人  

---

## 主要能做什么？

### 1. 思维画布  
把想法变成可视化的**节点与连线**：支持项目列表、单项目内的方块与箭头、缩放与拖拽，方便梳理流程、大纲或关系。适合头脑风暴和轻量笔记。

### 2. 日程管理  
添加、编辑和删除**日程事件**，支持重复规则；可与思维画布中的内容关联提醒（按项目使用习惯操作即可）。数据在本地保存，登录后可随账号同步到其他设备。

### 3. 数字助理  
创建多个**助理角色**，与 AI 对话；支持复制单条内容、删除单条或批量删除记录，便于整理聊天历史。需要网络与后端服务即可使用大模型能力。

### 4. 局域网传输  
在同一网络下**发现附近设备**，向对方**发送文件或文本**，并查看传输记录。适合不打孔、不依赖云盘时的临时互传。（Windows 上若发现不到设备，可留意防火墙对 UDP 端口的放行说明。）

### 5. 账户与同步（可选）  
- **不登录也能用**：以「本地账户」使用上述功能，数据只存在本机。  
- **登录后**：思维、日程、助理等数据可在**多端合并与同步**；支持注册登录、退出与更换账户，本机可为多个账号保留独立数据副本，避免串号。
